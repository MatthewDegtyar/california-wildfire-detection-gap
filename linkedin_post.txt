Where NASA's FIRMS satellite wildfire product fails — California, 2020 fire season:

- < 10 ac fires: 3% detection rate (and these are 93% of all CA fires)
- 10–1,000 ac: 25%
- ≥ 1,000 ac: 80%

The gap below 10 acres is the addressable market for drones / loiter aircraft.

Write-up + code: github.com/MatthewDegtyar/california-wildfire-detection-gap
