97% of California wildfires contained under 10 acres never produce a single FIRMS satellite detection.

I quantified where satellite wildfire detection fails across the 2020 CA fire season, and therefore where aerial detection (drones, persistent loiter) adds the most marginal value.

Methodology, calibrated model, gap-surface map, and the 12-page write-up:
github.com/MatthewDegtyar/california-wildfire-detection-gap

If you work in fire detection or autonomous aerial, I'd love to compare notes.

#wildfire #remotesensing #drones
